3 Approaches to Restructure Your Vault

Which mental model fits how you think?

A

Expanded PARA (keep what you have, fill the gaps)

Keep the numbered PARA backbone. Expand sub-folders to cover all life areas.

📥 00-Inbox
📁 01-Projects
  ├── Dev/
  ├── School/
  ├── Business/
  └── Career/
📁 02-Areas
  ├── Dev Stack/
  ├── School Subjects/
  └── Business/
📁 03-Resources
  ├── CS & Algorithms/
  ├── School Notes/
  ├── Business & Finance/
  └── Career/
📦 04-Archive
📋 Templates

Pros

  • Familiar — you already know PARA
  • Status-driven (active vs reference)

Cons

  • Still hard to know: Projects or Areas?
  • Folder count grows fast
B

Domain-First (browse by life area)

Top level = life domains. Inside each, you have active work + notes + reference.

📥 00-Inbox
💻 Dev/
  ├── Projects/
  ├── Algorithms & DS/
  └── Tech Notes/
🎓 School/
  ├── Courses/
  ├── Assignments/
  └── Study Notes/
💼 Business/
  ├── Projects/
  └── Research/
🚀 Career/
📦 Archive/
📋 Templates

Pros

  • Super clear where things go
  • Scales naturally per domain

Cons

  • Hard to see all active work at once
  • Cross-domain notes are awkward
C

Hybrid PARA + Domains ⭐ Recommended

PARA for status at the top. Domains as sub-folders. Best of both worlds.

📥 00-Inbox
📁 01-Projects
  ├── Dev/
  ├── School/
  └── Business/
📁 02-Areas (ongoing responsibilities)
  ├── Dev Stack/
  ├── Academics/
  └── Business/
📚 03-Knowledge (replaces Resources)
  ├── CS — Algorithms/
  ├── CS — Data Structures/
  ├── CS — System Design/
  ├── CS — Tech Concepts/
  ├── School — [Subject]/
  ├── Business & Finance/
  ├── Career & Jobs/
  ├── AI & Agents/
  └── Inspiration/
📦 04-Archive
📋 Templates

Pros

  • PARA logic — always know active vs reference
  • Domains inside = instantly clear where things go
  • Knowledge base grows cleanly without touching Projects
  • Easy to browse AND search

Cons

  • Slight refactor of current structure
